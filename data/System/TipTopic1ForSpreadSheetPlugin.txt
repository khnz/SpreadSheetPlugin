Over 80 formulae are available through the SpreadSheetPlugin.

For Example:
   * =$AVERAGE()=
   * =$IF()=
   * =$REPLACE()=
   * =$TIME()=

A formula can be placed anywhere in a topic, not just in tables.

Example:

To space out WikiWords, write: 
%JQREQUIRE{"chili"}%<verbatim class="tml">%CALC{"$PROPERSPACE(WikiGuest)"}%</verbatim>
*Result:*

%CALC{"$PROPERSPACE(WikiGuest)"}%

See [[SpreadSheetPlugin]] for more information
